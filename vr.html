Kitten Cannon
UNITY

My friend and I developed this game and released it on the Steam Store in October 2016 as part of a self-imposed one-month-long hackathon to build our experience. I worked on the Unity side of things, while my friend worked on the Blender side of things. We were also asked to demo at the annual Product Hunt meetup alongside Casper and Postmates, and brought out to NY to demo at TechCrunch Block Party.

Pointless Puppets
UNITY

Pointless Puppets lets you play with two puppets, pick up various objects and signs, and record silly GIFs on a beautifully painted puppet show stage. I worked with VR artist, Estella Tse, to develop this light-hearted piece - she painted everything in Tiltbrush, while I stitched her work together in Unity.
